What is a template?

When you save a template, we store:

  • Name (e.g. Standard, Staff, Staff Accounting)
  • Who it’s for (Members, Staff, or anyone)
  • Default? — auto-used when a new Member or new Staff is created
  • Which tools are YES (Accounting, Tickets, Worship, …)
