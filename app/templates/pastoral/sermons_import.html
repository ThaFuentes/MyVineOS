How MyVine structures a sermon
  1. Introduction — top only (opener / welcome)
  2. Section 1 — starts at Sermon Content / first main point
  3. Section 2, 3, … — each additional point or story
  4. Application (optional) then Conclusion (optional)
  5. Verses and Notes / Notes & References → the editor’s Notes & References box at the bottom (personal prep — not printed by default). Not a podium section.

Best Word layout: Title as Heading 1; Introduction / Sermon Content / Verses and Notes as Heading 2; inside the body use Point 1:, Point 2:, etc. AI mode only finds those breaks — it does not rewrite your words. Full scripture dumps and research always land in Notes & References.
