Visitors & public community

Who is not logged in gets the same kind of fine-grained checkboxes as people tools (see vs create vs comment on Prayers, Dreams, Prophecies, …).

Go to Members → Access → Visitors to set: e.g. Dreams = see only (no comment, no submit); Prayers = see + submit + comment.

Turning on a Church Office module here only makes it available for the church. People still only see those tiles if they have that tool under People tools.
